DRY MARTINI

Description:

The key to a great martini is using good quality gin and vermouth. The martini a countless variations drink, like the Vodka Martini and the Reverse Martini. However, we like to to stir it in five parts gin to one part vermouth, with a dash of orange bitter ties. Unlike in fiction, the martini is meant to be stirred, to balance the ingredients and properly dilute them.

Ingredients:

  • 4 ounces gin
  • 1 ounce dry vermouth
  • 1 dash orange bitters
  • Garnish: lemon twist

Steps:

  1. Add the gin, dry vermouth and orange bitters into a mixing glass with ice.
  2. Stir it untill very cold. For at least 20-30 seconds.
  3. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  4. Garnish with a lemon twist.
